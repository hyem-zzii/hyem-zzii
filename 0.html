Aliens

The Squeeze Toy Aliens (a.k.a. Little Green Men or LGMs for short) are rubber squeak toys in the Toy Story franchise.

They represent small, short, lime-skinned space aliens with three eyes, three-fingered hands chubby bodies with short arms
and legs, large feet, and oblong heads with pointy ears and a single club-tipped antenna on top.

All have the same appearance, speak in the same voice, and wear the blue space uniforms containing
the Pizza Planet logo on the right side of the chest and a purple collar around the neck.

They also wear a dark blue belt around their waists and a set of dark blue boots on their feet.
